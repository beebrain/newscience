Bachelor of Science Program in Information Technology

A rigorous 4-year, 128-credit program designed to develop analytical excellence and technical innovation in the next generation of IT leaders.

Philosophy & Objectives

Our program is founded on the belief that a strong theoretical foundation in applied mathematics and computer science is essential for mastering modern information technology. We aim to produce graduates who are not only technically proficient but also possess the analytical skills to solve complex problems in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.
